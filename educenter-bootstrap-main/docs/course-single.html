About Course

The Photography course at Educenter is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of both the technical and creative aspects of photography. Whether you're a beginner or an enthusiast looking to enhance your skills, this course offers hands-on experience with professional equipment, guided photo sessions, and expert-led workshops. From mastering camera settings to understanding lighting, composition, and post-production, you'll gain the confidence to create powerful visual stories. By the end of the course, youâ€™ll have developed a strong portfolio that reflects your personal style and photographic vision.

Requirements

  • Basic knowledge of using a digital camera.
  • Access to a DSLR or mirrorless camera (optional, provided in class).
  • Motivation to learn and practice regularly.
  • Passion for visual storytelling and creativity.
  • Ability to attend field trips and on-site photo shoots.
  • Basic computer skills for editing sessions.
  • Notebook for notes and project planning.
  • No prior formal education in photography required.

How to Apply

  • Complete the online application form on the Educenter website.
  • Upload a short motivation letter explaining your interest in photography.
  • Submit any sample photos if available (optional).
  • Wait for confirmation and attend an online orientation session.
  • Pay the registration fee to confirm your spot in the course.

Fees and Funding

The total cost for the Photography course is $450, which includes access to learning materials, workshops, and photo editing software during class sessions. Flexible payment plans are available upon request. Scholarships and partial funding may be available for outstanding applicants or those with financial need. Contact our admissions team for more details on eligibility and application procedures.
